H2020 EO Big Data Hackathon Nov 7-8

Written on October 7, 2019 by Edzer Pebesma.

openEO will participate, along with the four other H2020 projects funded under the same call, in the H2020 EO Big Data Hackathon, which is open for participation.

The hackaton will take place on Nov 7 and 8, 2019, in Frascati, Italy.
